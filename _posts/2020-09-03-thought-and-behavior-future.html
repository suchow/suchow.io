- What decision rule, based on _t_now and _t_begin, will produce the most accurate estimate of _t_end? --- # The delta _t_ argument, prediction - Given this, what should be predict about _t_end? --- # The delta _t_ argument, conclusion - The best prediction for how long an event will last into the future is how long it has lasted thus far, Î”_t_. ??? The major takeaway concepts here are that probability can be used to describe our uncertainty, but making a decision requires us to resolve that uncertainty in some way. --- # Optimal predictions in everyday cognition Say we come across a person who is 20 years old.
