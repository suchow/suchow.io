These maxims (or, more precisely, their violation) form the basis for inferences that we draw in conversation, which Grice called implicatures (to distinguish them from formal logical implications) Grice asserted that different ways of violating these maxims give rise to different types of implicatures. --- # Maxim of quantity. - Make your contribution as informative as is required. - Do not make your contribution more informative than is required.
